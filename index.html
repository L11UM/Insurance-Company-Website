About Us

My Introduction

Bruce Thompson founded Thompson Energy Insurance Services in 2013 after 20 years in the propane and petroleum insurance industry. This has created a passion for the energy industry that propels TEIS into the successful company it is today. With board memberships at top Gas Associations around the West Coast and the country, Bruce has a robust connection to the energy industry and has been a house name for over two decades. Bruce currently lives in Scottsdale, Arizona with his wife and seven children.
